It's Happening

Believe it or not, TXJS is one of the oldest JavaScript conferences around. While some folks would have you believe it started in 1997, it actually goes back to 2010. Back when jQuery 1.4 was still fresh, and when Facebook was just mediocre at Open Source JavaScript.

TXJS is a single track conference featuring some of the most interesting speakers on JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and UX. All skill levels are welcome. The conference will be held at the famous Paramount Theatre in downtown Austin.

If you're also interested in pre-conference training, Bocoup will be running a pre-conference Node.js workshop.
