It's Happening

Believe it or not, TXJS is one of the oldest JavaScript conferences around. While some folks would have you believe it started in 1997, it actually goes back to 2010. Back when jQuery 1.4 was still fresh, and when Facebook was just mediocre at Open Source JavaScript.

TXJS is a single track conference featuring some of the most interesting speakers on JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and UX. All skill levels are welcome. The conference will be held at the famous Paramount Theatre in downtown Austin.

If you're also interested in pre-conference training, Bocoup will be running Roost, their two-day intensive course on web development on the days leading up to TXJS.

We're releasing some tickets early due to demand, but keep an eye on this page and @txjs on Twitter for more information on additional speakers and extra-curricular events.

Code of Conduct

TXJS is fun and we expect you'll have a lot of fun, but it's also a professional conference. We adhere to the JSConf Code of Conduct. We will have clearly designated and safe reporting structures for conduct concerns during all conference events.
